Buy with confidence and clarity

Say goodbye to buyer’s remorse

“VALUEONSITE” is a revolutionary software product designed to model a “best overall value” sales transaction in a market place characterized by rising interest rates and inflation. The result is in total contrast to Oscar Wilde's well known quip that “there are those who know the price of everything and the value of nothing”.

Analysis Overview

Based on a transformative evaluation methodology and proprietary algorithms we take data input by the user to compare competing products or services. To provide maximum utility we have structured the product to address the transactional needs of both buyers and sellers as they participate in the market place. While there are notional differences between the two the fact is that in the final analysis they can be fairly described as two sides of the same coin.

A buyer can engage in market research analysis necessary to arrive at an optimized purchase decision or confirm that a decision ostensibly already made is the right choice.. Conversely the seller can apply the same process to determine how their product or service stacks up against their competitors.

Whatever the case the user is able to employ objective criteria to make an informed decision as opposed to relying on guess work or unreliable subjective assumptions. In mathematical terms our product can be described as utilizing a weighted decision matrix which may sound complicated but from a practical standpoint it is as simple as 1,2,3.

What you get

In an environment cluttered with sophisticated marketing strategies that are often confusing and In some cases misleading "VALUEONSITE” PROVIDES CLEAR, CONCISE AND COMPREHENSIVE COMPARISON ANALYSIS RESULTS. Lists Prices are compared to software generated Value Prices as defined by weighted price and product rating metrics. Differences are immediately apparent and are further amplified by the calculation of a product or service score.

But why stop there?

There will be occasions where the user may still prefer an item whose List Price is greater than the Value Price (VP) calculated by “VALUEONSITE”. Accordingly, our product conducts the sensitivity analysis that identifies the changes required to price weighting or product rating to make both Value and List Price equal. Given that “VALUEONSITE” is a modeling exercise and therefore indicative in nature, variances to some degree can be expected and the user can judge whether or not they are reasonable.

To further exploit the capabilities of our product the user will have the ability to conduct an abbreviated analysis of a transaction decision ostensibly already made. Typically this will occur for those items that are of low dollar value and as such may not warrant intensive scrutiny in terms of decision making. Notwithstanding, the ability to do what amounts to a quick price check will provide a level of comfort for the user in terms of dispensing with any lingering doubts a buyer may experience after a transaction is concluded.

One last word

Setting aside the benefits that accrue from being able to make an informed decision, use of our product will translate into the peace of mind for the user as a result of:

  • Being empowered by an analysis tool that is going to make the purchase decision easier and smarter;

  • Being engaged through the process of simple user inputs; and

  • Being excited at the prospect of knowing you have made a wise and informed decision, making “buyer remorse” a thing of the past!
