MAKING INFORMED DECISIONS IN AN UNCERTAIN MARKET PLACE

Introduction

Welcome to the introduction and demonstration of "VALUEWAND", a revolutionary software product designed to identify the product/service that provides the "best overall value" among competing options. The process is based on the use of an easily understood decision matrix table requiring minimal user inputs. The net result is in total contrast to Oscar Wilde's well known quip "that there are those who know the price of everything and the value of nothing".

Analysis Overview

Despite the notional differences between buyers and sellers, they can be fairly described as two sides of the same coin. They both intuitively assess the quality and associated price of a product/service that will maximize the benefits that accrue to them. While subjective in nature this process is not without merit. We employ the same evaluation criteria with our proprietary algorithms and methodologies to produce objective and measurable outcomes. This transactional analysis allows buyers and sellers to participate with confidence in a market place characterized by rising interest rates and inflation.

To put it succinctly if there ever was a product for our times this is it. Buyer's remorse will be profoundly mitigated through market analysis, or, by corroborating a purchase decision ostensibly already made. Sellers on the other hand can discern price points that satisfy competitive requirements. Moreover, all parties involved will enjoy the peace of mind that comes from engaging in a disciplined and objective analysis process that will enhance their ability to make informed decisions.

Conclusion

The ability to make smart money decisions is a key component of financial literacy. Our product is fundamental to achieving this goal. It is our intention to award an exclusive perpetual software license to an organization that can make this product widely available On the surface at least financial institutions are obvious candidates. However, our current focus is on select fintech companies who may view our product as being complimentary to existing product portfolios. Should that be the case we look forward to your response.

To further amplify our assertion we have available an interactive demo which we invite you to access.
